# Findings

## Customer Overview

- Dataset contains more than 10,000 banking customers.
- Customers belong to multiple geographical regions.
- Both male and female customers are represented across all segments.

## Financial Insights

- Average customer balance is substantial across the dataset.
- Some customers maintain significantly higher balances than others.
- Balance distribution differs by country.

## Churn Insights

- Customer churn is present within the dataset.
- Active members generally show better retention.
- Age appears to influence customer exit behavior.

## Credit Score Insights

- Credit scores vary across customers.
- Credit score statistics help assess financial risk levels.

## Business Recommendation

- Increase engagement initiatives for inactive customers.
- Monitor customer segments with higher churn tendencies.
- Develop targeted retention strategies based on customer behavior.
